### Senior Case-Study Standard

Each project should answer:

1. What consequential product problem existed, and why did it matter?
2. What was Pravallika directly responsible for?
3. What constraints, ambiguity, or organizational complexity shaped the work?
4. Which insights changed the product direction?
5. What difficult decisions and tradeoffs were made?
6. How did design influence product, engineering, research, or business partners?
7. What changed for users or the business, supported by evidence?
8. What would be improved or explored next?

Avoid process-diary storytelling. Research and artifacts should appear only when they explain a meaningful decision.
